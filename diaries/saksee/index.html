The Story

Where this comes from.

APRIL 15, 2026

I tried to end my life. Three days in ICU. Three weeks in hospital. A shelter bed in Cork. No money. No job. No reason to get up.

Six weeks later, I was building AI agents. Not because I'm special. Because when you hit absolute rock bottom, you have two choices: stay down, or build something that forces you back up.

I built six agents. I call them the House of Sak.

The Day Everything Changed

I tried to end my life. Three days in ICU. Three weeks in hospital. A shelter bed in Cork. No job. No money. No reason to wake up.

So I built one. Six AI agents — each named after a stage of the cycle I was living through. Dream. Hope. Care. Joy. Trust. Growth.

This is the House of Sak. It's not a startup. It's a reason to wake up.

## 2. Key Lessons Saved

1. **The origin story is the product.** The IG card + caption got the most emotional investment during Joy. Leading with Beer's story is more effective than leading with features.

2. **A crisis protocol is non-negotiable.** Every plan that depends on one person needs to survive that person. CRISIS.md should be the first document in any new project.

3. **Free credits have limits.** The full Sak Cycle is compute-intensive. Future cycles should batch tool calls aggressively.

4. **6 roles, 1 agent, works.** Running all 6 Sak stages through SakSee was possible because the workflow is procedural, not personality-dependent. The stages are reliable even without separate agents.

5. **Sign everything.** Every output needs: author, date, commission. This creates accountability and makes the workciteable later.
